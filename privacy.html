Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our Services, and enhance your browsing experience. We use both session-based and persistent cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • 1.
    Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, including security, authentication, and basic functionality.
  • 2.
    Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our website, helping us understand user behavior and improve performance.
  • 3.
    Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide a personalized experience.
  • 4.
    Marketing Cookies: These cookies track your activity to deliver targeted advertisements and measure marketing campaign effectiveness.

Third-Party Tracking

We use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to track and analyze website usage. These services may use cookies and similar tracking technologies. Third-party advertisers may also place cookies on your device to deliver targeted advertisements. You can control many tracking technologies through your browser settings or opt-out tools provided by third parties.

Cookie Management

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically delete cookies or set your browser to refuse cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of our website. For more information about managing cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org or your browser's help section.
